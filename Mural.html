Sunflower Mural

I was asked to bring the side of this barn to life with a beautiful and vibrant mural of sunflowers.

Step One: Sketches and Planning
The clients knew that they wanted the mural to include large sunflowers that were vibrant and stuck out. From there, I started met with them to decide on colors and style.
Step Two: Sketch and prepare the canvas!
Next I used soap and water to ensure that the surface was clean and that the paint would stick to the siding. Once that is done, then I can begin to sketch out the composition with chalk.
Step Three: Get to Work!
First I began color blocking. I mixed my paint together to achieve the colors I wanted first. From there, I began to paint.
