Olney Outlaws

The Olney Outlaws is the LaSalle University’s new student section group, dedicated to creating an exciting and energetic gameday atmosphere. As the social media chair, I get to showcase my skills, designing posts and the Outlaws’ logo. It has been a unique opportunity to shape our identity and unite fans under a bold, spirited brand.

Through creative and engaging content, I promote upcoming games, rally student support, and highlight the passion and excitement of our fans.

Logo Brainstorm
When planning for this logo, I knew I wanted to use the traditional explorer with a Western outlaw flare. I took inspiration from the bandana covering the face with a cowboy style hat.
Next,
I played around with some half-tone shading but found that it would be too difficult for printing options. I also tried to take it a step further and depict an explorer on the back of a horse.
Final Design
After making adjustments to the color, vectors, and pose, I was able to develope a bold and recognizable image. The logo is featured on T-shirts and social media.
