2025 Holiday Card Collection

I created a handful of printed cards for family, clients and even La Salle University!

Inspired by the Starbuck's "Been There" series of mugs, I wanted something playful and reflective of the many activities and characteristics of my family. To give some of the objects context, I created a scavenger hunt to be used on the back of the card. They included fun hints and riddles such as "Find the place where summers are better and the water is always "cold.”

Custom Christmas Cards
This year’s custom Christmas cards were designed to feel personal and intentional, often reflecting each family’s personality or marking meaningful moments. Each card was hand-illustrated with the goal of turning a holiday greeting into a keepsake.
La Salle University Holiday Card


I was given the honor of creating La Salle's Holiday Card. I was asked to create a water color image of the grotto, a statue that is central to the campus. After some thought, I was inspired by an image I took of the grotto early in the morning. The sun was peaking through the trees behind and I thought about its resemblence to the holiday season. Both promise warmth and a new beginning so I made it the central focus of the design. I used a variety of digital brushes to obtain the watercolor effect and hand illustrated it using an Ipad.
