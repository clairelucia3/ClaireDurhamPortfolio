About Me


Hello, I'm Claire.


I am a dedicated artist and graphic designer with a keen eye for detail and a deep passion for creative design. I take pride in using my talents to not only bring ideas to life but also to create meaningful experiences that resonate with people. My work is driven by a desire to inspire joy and serve as a powerful tool in visual communication and marketing

I am a 2022 graduate of Napoleon High School and am currently a third-year student at La Salle University in Philadelphia, far from my small hometown in Northwest Ohio. As I near my graduation in 2026, I am so excited to begin the rest of my journey using my creative skills and talents.
