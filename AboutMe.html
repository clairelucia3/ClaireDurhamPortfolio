Hello, I'm Claire.
I’m a senior at La Salle University, studying both Art History and Digital Arts/Multimedia with Honors distinction. Blending these two fields has given me a unique perspective on how art has shaped culture through history and how design continues that story today. With that in mind, I create logos, content, and websites that are not only visually engaging but also meaningful, drawing on my background in both tradition and innovation.
From branding packages and logos to social media campaigns and content calendars, my process begins with understanding the needs of the audience and the identity of the client. I draw on my background in fine art, working with texture, color, and composition to bring depth and originality to digital design. Whether I’m developing a marketing system for a small business, creating custom graphics, or painting a family portrait, I strive to produce work that is clear, memorable, and adaptable across platforms.
