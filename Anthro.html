Great Pyranees Infographic

This infographic highlights the Great Pyrenees, a powerful yet gentle mountain breed known for its loyalty and protective instincts. Inspired by the dog’s origins in the Pyrenees Mountains between Spain and France, the design uses layered mountain silhouettes, a full moon, and a cool nighttime color palette to reflect both the breed’s environment and naturally nocturnal tendencies

The layout organizes information into clear, digestible sections—including history, physical traits, temperament, training, grooming, and health, to make the information accessible at a glance. I illustrated icons such as the brain, heart, brush and map to visually reinforce each category, creating a cohesive and engaging visual system.

Overall, the goal of this infographic was to balance educational clarity with strong visual storytelling—creating a design that is both informative and approachable for potential dog owners or anyone interested in learning about the Great Pyrenees
